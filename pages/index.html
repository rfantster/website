In The Beginning

I was born in Winnfield, Louisiana. Don't remember much of the first couple of years, but I think we moved to New Orleans when I was about 1 or 2 years old.
I must have been about 2 at the time of this photo.

My Friend, Lightning

Not sure of this date.
However, While in the New Orleans area, my dad and I used to ride horses a lot. This is a picture of my brothers, Louis and Johnny, on the back of my pony, Lightning. I gave him the name Lightning for a valid reason, he and I ran many races against adults and their horses and seldom lost. We also competed in numerous horse-shows, running barrels, stakes, and keyhole competitions, again, always running against adults and horses (not ponies).
